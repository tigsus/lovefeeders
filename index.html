A Dog's Yada

The Plastic Heart

Step into the captivating world of "A Dog's Yada," where the unlikely anti-hero Chobak embarks on an extraordinary journey with Dean and a rich tapestry of quirky characters spanning the heavenly, soul, and earthly realms. This book is more than just a story; it’s a revival of faith and self-discovery.

Inspired by the Hebrew word “yada,” meaning “intimate knowledge,” this enchanting narrative beckons you to deepen your relationship with God. For those hungry for the Word, this modern epic of discipleship and evangelism invites you to contemplate and grow in foundational truths and the supernatural in the Age of Artificial Intelligence.

Debut author Jaret Pfluger deftly weaves poetic insights with Scriptural wisdom, guiding readers through the complexities and struggles of the human heart. Prepare to triumph through the Lord Jesus Christ and grow closer to God. You, too, can declare with Chobak, “Who would have guessed that being chummy with the Almighty could be a hoot?”

Embrace this opportunity to deepen your faith and connection with God. Start your journey today!
